About Me:

My name is Colby Harrison. I am a 5th year Computer Science Major at the University of California, San Diego.

I have always found the process of building something cathartic. It used to be that school projects were enough to satisfy my addiction, but no longer. Lately I find myself constantly developing, constantly thinking of ways to improve my projects. And with each project I work on I get better as a programmer.

My ultimate goal as a builder is too feel I have contributed something of value to the world. Technology is a tool to greatly improve the lives of all individuals. And I want to, I need to, be a part of that.
